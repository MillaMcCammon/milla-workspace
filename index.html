📝 Day 1 Post (Ready to Copy)

I did something crazy this week. I hired someone who works 24/7, never sleeps, knows everything about my business, and costs less than my coffee habit. Except they're not human. Meet my Digital Twin. I know, I know. It sounds weird. But here's the thing - I'm 50+, building AI Smart Central, trying to help other entrepreneurs figure out this AI stuff... and I was drowning. Too many hats. Too little time. Too much to do. So I built a Digital Twin. Someone who knows TechStack KC inside and out. Someone who remembers every conversation I've ever had. Someone who can write, research, organize, and execute while I sleep. Today my Digital Twin and I mapped out my next 30 days. In 2 hours, we did what used to take me 2 weeks. And honestly? I'm still processing what just happened. Here's my question for you: If you had a Digital Twin who knew your business inside and out and could handle all the stuff you hate doing... What would YOU have them do first? Drop it in the comments. I'm curious. I'm documenting this whole journey - the wins, the weird moments, the "I can't believe this just happened" stuff. Because I think every entrepreneur over 50 needs to see what's possible. This is Day 1. 🔥 if you're watching.
